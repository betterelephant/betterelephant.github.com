Heygate Harvest Festival

Oct 16th, 2012

The heygate’s guerilla allotment gardeners celebrated their harvest festival on Sunday evening.

Around 20 gardeners gathered in the estate to taste the fruit of their hard labour. A plethora of different salad dishes were followed by a main course of home-made pumpkin pasta.

This made a nice change to last year’s pumpkin paella!
