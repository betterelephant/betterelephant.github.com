Better Elephant is a community-led group exploring proposals for an alternative and more sustainable regeneration of the Elephant & Castle.

“There are failed estates but this is not one of them.”

Tim Tinker 2011Heygate Estate Original Architect

We believe that the current development model being proposed by developers Lend Lease ltd. is not environmentally sustainable and has become outdated. It was conceived more than 15 years ago and fails to address the new global challenges of climate change, economic hardship and shrinking supplies of cheap energy. We are creating a more environmentally, socially and economically sustainable development plan, as a co-ordinated local response to the current environmental and economic crisis.

The new Localism Act that was passed on November 15 2011, introduced new rights and powers to allow local communities to shape local planning developments by preparing neighbourhood plans.

Better Elephant is currently helping to draft a neighbourhood plan for the Elephant & Castle area, which explores alternative options to full-scale demolition of the Heygate estate. Proposals include retaining the ‘urban forest’ of trees inside the estate, a community-led refurbishment of some existing buildings and local food growing initiatives.

We are planning to publish the draft version of our neighbourhood plan in Summer 2012 for consultation and feedback from the wider community.

Local residents and key professionals who are interested in the future of our neighbourhood and environment, are invited to get involved and share in our inspiring vision of the future.
