BETTER ELEPHANT is a community-led campaign seeking to improve the regeneration of the Elephant & Castle.



Our campaign is a response to the current masterplan proposals for the area. The local community has raised a number of significant concerns involving housing, environmental and financing issues:

Housing-> The significant planned reduction in family-size homes and housing for people on low incomes. Read more..

Environmental-> The loss of a significant number of trees and green space, and the potentially devastating carbon impact of the current scheme.

Financing-> The current scheme’s financial impact on council funds at a time of unprecedented budget cuts.

Our vision of a community-led regeneration run by local residents, artists, businesses and community groups, will engage the local community in shaping the space they inhabit, and transform the Elephant into a unique and vibrant creative hub.

The new Localism Act, which was passed on November 15 2011, introduced new rights and powers to allow local communities to shape local planning law by coming together to prepare neighbourhood plans. Better Elephant has formed a neighbourhood forum and is preparing a neighbourhood plan for the Elephant & Castle.

Housing-> We are considering alternatives to demolition of the Heygate estate, including partial demolition/rebuild and the creation of a public park.

We are inviting local residents and key professionals who have an interest in the future of our neighbourhood and city, to get involved and join this important debate.
