BETTER ELEPHANT is a community-led campaign seeking to improve the regeneration of the Elephant & Castle.



Our campaign is a response to the recently-revealed masterplan proposals for the area. We believe that the current developer-led plans - based solely on market interests - will not benefit the local community and are not in the wider public interest.

Our vision of a community-led regeneration run by local residents, artists, businesses and community groups, will engage the local community in shaping the space they inhabit, and transform the Elephant into a unique and vibrant creative hub.

The Localism Act, which received Royal Assent on November 15 2011, introduced new rights and powers to allow local communities to shape new developments by coming together to prepare neighbourhood plans. Better Elephant has formed a neighbourhood forum and is preparing a neighbourhood plan for the Elephant & Castle based around the following proposals.

  • Housing-> We are considering alternatives to demolition of the Heygate estate, including partial demolition/rebuild and the creation of a public park. Read more….

  • Transport-> We are considering proposals for the significant transformation of the northern roundabout, as part of a car-free pedestrian and cyclist-friendly development with designated cycle lanes and a 20mph speed limit.Read more…

  • Environment-> We are looking at creating a public park within the footprint of the Heygate estate, on the site of the existing Urban Forest and Ecological Park. We are also looking at the feasibility of installing a biomass boiler to power the existing district-heating system on the Heygate and Salisbury Row estates.Read more…

If you support our plan for regeneration you can sign our petition, or if you would like to get involved in shaping the space of your community, then please do come along to one of our meetings.
