Financial cost of the current scheme

It is estimated that the total financial cost of decanting and securing the Heygate has amounted to over £45 million.

The estate is currently costing the council £22,000 per year for the estate lighting and £48,500 per month for private security.

The loss of rent has now amounted to over £25 million, and is accruing at rate of approx. £100,000 per week.

The cost of simple repair back in 1998 was £7.2 million. A survey was undertaken by the council in 1998, which asked Heygate residents what their preferences were in relation to several different options of demoltion/refurbishment/rebuilding the estate. 63% of the respondents stated that they would rather just see the estate undergo this simple option of repair.
